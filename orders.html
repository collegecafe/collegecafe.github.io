Orders

.

College Cafe is currently accepting orders before lunchtime. To order bring a container and cutlery to the drop-off area during recess on Thursday. The drop-off area is inside, near the staff room.

Come to the door near the tennis courts at the start of lunch to collect your food.

Anyone who orders food will recieve a $1.00 discount.
